Which Group Has the Highest Suicide Rate among Different Factors?

     Suicide rate analysis (2005 - 2015) HELLO (kaggle dataset) by Yu-Chieh Chen, Yijun Liu

    The graphs show that
  • Among countries, East Europe, Central Asia, Japan and South Korea have highest suicide rates.
  • Among ages, 35-54 year-old people tend to have highest suicide rates comparing to other ages.
    • However, uncertainty still remains. For example, the data does not contain all the countries. Therefore, there might be some countries having higher suicide rates.
